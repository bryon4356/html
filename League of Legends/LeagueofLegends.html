League of Legends

League of Legends is an online PC game that was made in 2009. This game is what is known as a MOBA which is a massive online battle arena game style where five players aside try and take down each other's base to win the game. The teams are made up of champions and there are over 140 different choices to choose from. For more information visit Leagueoflegends.com!

Champions

With over 140 champions to select from in League of Legends there is something for everyone to play. From Bruiser's to Assassian's you can play the game anyway you want with very little repetition from game to game. Along with having over 140 champions there are over 1200 skins to choose from for these chamions to allow you as the player to have choice in how your champion loooks and talks in game. These skins also allow for better visuals with their abilities. To find your favorite champion visit Leagueoflegends.com!

How to Play

There are three lanes on the map. Top, Mid and Bottom. Top and Mid lane have a one versus one against another player. Bottom has a two versus two where it is an attack damage carry and a support champion laning against another duo with the same style of champions. The fifth player of each team plays in the jungle on the map where they fight against jungle camps where it is computer controlled charcters that are easy to kill and give you buffs and experience. In the lanes it is the goal to take the towers down and make gold from killing minions that are computer conrolled. The team that wins the game is to the team that has the most gold and has taken down the most towers.

E-Sports

League of Legends has one of the biggest playing populations in the world with over ten million active players daily. With that amount of players there is a need for a professional scene. There are over ten pro leagues in the world that have the best players playing against each other to become their own regions champion. Each region's champion will then play against other region champions in the two major events the Mid-Season Invitational and the LOL World Championship. The current world champion is DRX from Korea which was hosted in the United States and Mexico. To see more information about LOL E-sports visit lolesports.com!
